Who is Hephaestus?

Hephaestus is the divine smith of Olympus, master of fire, metal, and craftsmanship. He forges weapons, armor, and wondrous creations for gods and heroes.

Though often portrayed as physically impaired, he is unmatched in technical skill and creative ingenuity. His workshops symbolize persistence, precision, and transformative craft.

Personality

Hephaestus is diligent, inventive, and resilient. He turns hardship into mastery through focus and craft.
