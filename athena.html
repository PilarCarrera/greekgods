Who is Athena?

Athena is the goddess of wisdom, strategic warfare, and skilled craftsmanship. Born fully armed from Zeus head, she represents intellect guided by discipline and reason.

She is patron of Athens and a guardian of heroes, often guiding them with practical counsel rather than brute force. Athena is linked with civilization, law, and inventive skill.

  • Support of Perseus
  • The weaving contest with Arachne

Personality

Athena is composed, analytical, and principled. She values foresight, self-control, and intelligent action over reckless aggression.

Wisdom is Athena strongest weapon.
