Who is Demeter?

Demeter is the goddess of grain, harvest, and fertile earth. She governs agriculture and the rhythms that sustain human life.

Her bond with her daughter Persephone explains the cycle of the seasons in Greek myth. Demeter grief brings winter, and Persephone return restores growth.

  • Torch

  • Poppy

Famous Myths

  • The abduction of Persephone
  • Founding of the Eleusinian Mysteries
  • Her wandering in grief
  • Blessing Triptolemus with agriculture

Personality

Demeter is nurturing, steadfast, and deeply maternal. Her love is life-giving, and her sorrow can reshape the world.

Demeter feeds the world through sacred cycles.
