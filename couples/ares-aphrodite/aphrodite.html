Who is Aphrodite?

Aphrodite is the goddess of love, beauty, and desire. Her influence moves gods and mortals alike, shaping attraction, passion, and emotional longing.

In many traditions she is born from sea foam, arriving with irresistible grace. Her stories reveal both the delight and turmoil that love can bring.

Personality

Aphrodite is enchanting, persuasive, and emotionally powerful. She inspires devotion and jealousy in equal measure.

Aphrodite stirs every heart she touches.
