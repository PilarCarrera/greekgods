Who is Ares?

Ares is the god of violent war and the raw fury of battle. Unlike Athena strategic warfare, Ares represents direct conflict, intensity, and the destructive side of combat.

Though feared for his ferocity, he is not always favored among the Olympians. His myths highlight both his martial power and the consequences of unchecked aggression.

Famous Myths

  • Capture by the Aloadae giants
  • Role in the Trojan War

Personality

Ares is impulsive, passionate, and combative. He thrives in action and confrontation, often acting before reflection.

Ares marches where battle calls loudest.
