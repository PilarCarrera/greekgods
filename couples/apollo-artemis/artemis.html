Who is Artemis?

Artemis is the goddess of the hunt, wild animals, and untamed places. Twin sister of Apollo, she is often depicted with a bow, moving swiftly through forests and mountains.

She is also associated with the moon and with protection of young life. Artemis fiercely guards her independence and the natural world under her care.

Personality

Artemis is independent, swift, and uncompromising. She is protective toward the vulnerable and relentless against disrespect.

The moonlit wilds belong to Artemis.
