Who is Apollo?

Apollo is a radiant Olympian god associated with light, music, prophecy, healing, and archery. As lord of Delphi, he speaks through oracles and reveals divine insight.

He is a patron of poetry and harmony, yet his myths also show a god whose brilliance can be both life-giving and severe. Apollo embodies order, beauty, and divine measure.

Symbols & Attributes

Famous Myths

  • Slaying of Python at Delphi
  • His love for Daphne
  • Musical contest with Marsyas
  • Guiding the walls of Troy with Poseidon

Personality

Apollo is refined, disciplined, and exacting. He represents clarity and truth, but he can be unforgiving when challenged.

Apollo brings light to prophecy and song.
