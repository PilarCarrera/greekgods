Who is Hera?

Hera is the queen of the Olympian gods and the goddess of marriage, family, and royal authority. She is the wife and sister of Zeus and is often shown as a majestic, dignified figure seated on a throne.

As protector of lawful unions, Hera represents the sanctity of marriage and the stability of the household. Many myths portray her fierce defense of her status and her relentless pursuit of justice against betrayal.

Personality

Hera is proud, strategic, and deeply protective of her honor. She can be nurturing and regal, but she is also formidable when wronged.

Hera guards the sacred bond of marriage.
