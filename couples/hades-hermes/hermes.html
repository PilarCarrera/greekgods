Who is Hermes?

Hermes is the swift messenger of the gods, patron of travelers, merchants, and clever speech. He moves freely between divine and mortal worlds, carrying messages and guiding souls.

Known for wit and adaptability, Hermes is also linked with boundaries, exchange, and invention. His myths celebrate speed, intelligence, and playful cunning.

Symbols & Attributes

  • Caduceus
  • Winged Sandals
  • Winged Hat
  • Tortoise Lyre

Famous Myths

  • Stealing Apollo cattle as an infant
  • Guiding Perseus and Odysseus
  • Leading souls to the underworld
  • Inventing the lyre

Personality

Hermes is quick-thinking, persuasive, and resourceful. He thrives on movement, negotiation, and clever solutions.
