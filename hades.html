Who is Hades?

Hades rules the underworld, the realm of the dead, and governs the order of afterlife domains. Though often feared, he is not evil but stern and just in his duties.

He is also associated with hidden wealth, since precious metals and fertile seeds come from beneath the earth. His kingdom is disciplined, shadowed, and absolute.

Personality

Hades is reserved, authoritative, and unyielding. He values order and boundaries over spectacle.

Hades keeps the balance beyond mortal life.
